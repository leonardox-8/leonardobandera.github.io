Research in Progress

A Solvable Model of Generative Diffusion and the Memorization Phenomenon

Manuscript in preparation (with C. Lucibello, F. Elgorni, B. Annesi)

We study the training dynamics of score-based diffusion models in analytically tractable random-feature architectures. Using tools from random matrix theory and spectral analysis, we identify distinct learning regimes governed by eigenvalue transitions of the empirical Gram matrix and relate them to memorization phenomena.

Detailed derivations and code will be released upon submission.
